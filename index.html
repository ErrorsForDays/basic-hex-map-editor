Controls

  • Left click and drag to move map.
  • Middle click and drag to rotate map.
  • Mouse wheel to zoom.
  • Click a tile to select it.
  • Add new tiles to the map by selecting a tile at the edge, then click new tile.
  • Click and drag the blue arrows above the selected tile to change its height.
  • The clone tool from the menu makes editing big maps much quicker.
  • Edit or add materials from the materials list in the menu.
  • Red arrow indicates the center tile. Double click a tile to make it the center tile.

Tip: Menu

The menu button is in the top right corner.
Use it to see the controls, import/export maps, or change settings.
